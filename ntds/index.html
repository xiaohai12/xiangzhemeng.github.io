Random Network
The graph present all the links among all the Macbook and Surface related products. If there are plenty of connections among several products, the position of these products will be very close and from a stable cluster.
In order to verify if the prducts in one cluster are related to the same original product, we use different color to present Macbook related products(dark blue) and Surface related products(light blue).

Circle Network
The left part of the circle are Surface related products and the right part are Macbook related products. Most links connect the nodes situated at the same side of the circle. There are only a few links coming cross the circle from one side to the other side.
Hence, Surface related products and Macbook related products form two relatively apparent clusters in the network.
